I am a third-year Ph.D student in Computer Engineering at Northwestern University, advised by Prof. Qi Zhu. I also work close with Prof. Alfred Chen. Prior to Northwestern, I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2019.

The long term goal of my research is to make machine learning components in autonomous driving safe, interpretable and efficient. My research interests include general machine learning and autonomous driving(from perception, prediction to decision making). Recent works focus on robustness and generative methods in deep learning applications
