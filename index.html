I am a final-year Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at Northwestern University, advised by Prof. Qi Zhu. I also work close with Prof. Alfred Chen. Prior to Northwestern, I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Zhejiang University in 2019.

The long-term goal of my research is to make machine learning components in autonomous systems robust, interpretable, and efficient. My research interests include robust and explainable ML, generative models for behavior modeling/decision-making, and their applications in embodied AI and autonomous systems.
